Our Story

A Century of Refined Hospitality

Founded in 1923 by the Beaumont family, Villa Soleil has welcomed the world's most discerning travelers to its sun-drenched terraces and elegant suites for over a century.

We believe luxury is not about extravagance — it's about the effortless quality of each moment: the morning light through linen curtains, the scent of lavender in the evening breeze.
