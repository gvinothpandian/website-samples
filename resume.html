UX Design Approach

Design Process & Methodology
  • Discover: Stakeholder interviews, user surveys, and competitive analysis to define goals and pain points.
  • Define: Affinity mapping, user personas, and job-to-be-done frameworks to synthesise research into actionable insights.
  • Design: Low-fidelity sketches → information architecture → interactive Figma prototypes with component libraries.
  • Validate: Moderated and unmoderated usability tests, A/B experiments, and heuristic evaluation (Nielsen's 10 heuristics).
  • Deliver: Developer handoff via Figma with annotated specs, design tokens, and accessibility documentation.
