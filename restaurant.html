Our Story

Four generations
of genuine cooking

Founded by Nonna Rosa in 1987, Osteria Luce began as a tiny trattoria with six tables and one dream: to feed people the way a family feeds each other — with patience, generosity, and soul.

Today, the kitchen is led by her grandson Marco, who trained in Naples, Bologna, and New York before returning to honour his grandmother's recipes with modern technique.
