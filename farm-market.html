Our Story

Grown with love,
not chemicals

"We believe the best food comes from the most honest hands — people who know their soil like an old friend."

Fieldroot was born in 2017 when a group of small farmers and food lovers realized that the distance from farm to table had grown too long, too complicated, and too cold.

We partner exclusively with family farms within 200 miles, paying above-market prices and building relationships that last seasons, not just transactions.

— Margaret & Tom Fields, Founders
